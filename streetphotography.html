STREET PHOTOGRAPHY

Fashion is a personal hobby of mine, and I'm fascinated by the diverse styles and opinions people have on it. Earlier this year, I decided to channel this interest creatively by going around central London and photographing individuals whose fashion choices caught my eye. I plan to continue this project and I'm currently brainstorming ways to expand it beyond just taking photos.
