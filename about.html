PROFILE

My name is Smulla, also known as Shaun. I'm a graduate in Video Games Art & Design from Royal Holloway, University of London. I'm 21 years old and currently live in East London. I'm a self-proclaimed artist who is constantly seeking opportunities to expand and express creativity. My passion for learning drives me to master various forms of art, whether it’s games, 2D, 3D, animation, photography and beyond. If I don’t know how to do something, I’ll learn it. I firmly believe that with determination and the vast resources available on the internet, anyone can learn to do anything. This is how I've created everything I have ever made.
