JOJI - DIE FOR YOU (A FAN-MADE MUSIC VIDEO)

This music video was created as a university project. I directed, edited and acted in the music video. Prior to this, I had little experience in videography, especially music videos. I had to teach myself the entire process, with some assistance from friends, in cinematography and as a female lead.

CLICK TO WATCH
Behind the Process
I chose "Die For You" because I liked the song (obviously) and its messages, which sparked a plethora of creative ideas. Some of these, like the burning photos during the chorus, materialised beautifully, while others didn’t, with some not making the final cut. Such as a scene where I was in a blood bath, didn’t translate well on screen and were ultimately discarded. I drew inspiration from Joji’s past music videos and the song’s original video. Overall, it was an incredibly rewarding project, with lots of lessons learned and I’m eager to create another one where I can solely focus on directing and editing, without the added responsibility of acting.
Production Details
  • Camera: Canon EOS 250D
  • Lens: Canon EF-s 18-55mm f/4-5.6 IS STM
  • Lights: Aladdin 2x1
  • Stabilization: Tripod
  • Software: Adobe Photoshop & Premiere Pro
  • Crew: Alex Tach-Karim & Ai Kaneko
