VFX

The latest skill I've picked up, 3D modelling objects and integrating them into real-world environments using Blender. Below, you can find a list of all the projects I've been involved in.

Ole & Steen VFX Video
This project was made in collaboration with Oskar (oskar.edit). We conceptualised the idea purely to test and have fun with our creativity. I was responsible for 3D modelling and texturing, a recreation of the Ole & Steen coffee cup, as well as the “actor” for the video. Whilst Oskar handled the cinematography and editing with Keys (stopmotionkeys) as an assistant.
Production Details

Below is the equipment and software I used:

  • Camera: Sony FX3
  • Lens: Sigma 24-70
  • Stabilization: Weebills Gimbal
  • Software: Adobe After Effects, Photoshop, Premiere Pro & Blender
  • Crew: okedits & stopmotionkeys
